What is Systems Design Engineering?

↬Systems Design Engineering is one of the most broad Engineering programs at the University of Waterloo.

↬ It allows students to not only learn a lot of the math and sciences behind many Engineering jobs, but the program also focuses on how to design things with the user in mind.

↬ It's the only Engineering program in Canada that has a class that focuses on Human Factors in Engineering.

↬ There is a design course every school term, some of which are focused on one big group project.

↬ The class size is around 90 people, and we take almost all of our classes together, most in the same classroom.
